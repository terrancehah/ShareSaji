## 1. Project Vision

ShareSaji is a web-based viral marketing platform that helps Malaysian local restaurants increase foot traffic and sales through multi-level discount sharing. By turning customers into brand ambassadors, restaurants achieve measurable ROI without upfront marketing costs.

**Tagline:** *"Share the savings, grow the community"*

---

## 2. The Problem

### Current Challenges for Malaysian Restaurants

1. **High Customer Acquisition Costs**
   - Traditional advertising (Facebook ads, flyers) costs RM500-2,000/month
   - Low conversion rates (1-3%)
   - Difficult to measure ROI

2. **One-Time Discounts Don't Drive Loyalty**
   - First-time discounts (e.g., 10% off) attract customers once
   - No incentive for repeat visits
   - No mechanism for word-of-mouth growth

3. **Limited Viral Marketing Tools**
   - Referral programs exist but lack multi-level rewards
   - Customers have no ongoing incentive to share
   - Manual tracking is cumbersome

4. **Lack of Data-Driven Insights**
   - Owners don't know which promotions work
   - No visibility into customer referral networks
   - Can't optimize discount rates based on ROI

---

## 3. The Solution

### ShareSaji's Unique Value Proposition

**For Customers:**
- 🎟️ **Guaranteed 5% discount** on first visit after registration
- 💰 **Unlimited earning potential** through referrals (1% of downline spends)
- 🔄 **Passive income** from up to 3 levels of downlines
- 📱 **Simple sharing** via QR code, link, or code

**For Restaurants:**
- 📈 **Pay-for-performance** model (discounts only on actual sales)
- 🎯 **Measurable ROI** through real-time analytics
- 🚀 **Viral growth** without upfront marketing spend
- 🔧 **Configurable parameters** to optimize profitability

**For the Ecosystem:**
- 🌐 **Cross-restaurant network** (Phase 2) pools customer base
- 🤝 **Community-driven** growth benefits all participants
- 🔒 **Fraud-resistant** design (staff verification, expiry controls)

## 4. How It Works

### Customer Journey

```
1. DISCOVER
   Staff promotes: "Scan QR for 5% off today!"
   ↓
2. REGISTER
   Customer scans QR, enters email/birthday/age
   Gets unique referral code (e.g., SAJI-ABC123)
   ↓
3. FIRST DISCOUNT
   At checkout, shows code → Staff applies 5% discount
   ↓
4. SHARE
   Customer shares code on WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram
   ↓
5. EARN
   When downline spends RM100 → Customer earns RM1 (1%)
   Earnings from up to 3 levels of downlines
   ↓
6. REDEEM
   Next visit, customer redeems virtual currency (up to 20% of bill)
   ↓
7. REPEAT
   Continue sharing to earn more, creating viral loop
```

### Multi-Level Rewards Example

```
Root User spends RM100
├─ Gets: RM5 guaranteed discount
└─ Cost to restaurant: 5%

Level 1 (via Root) spends RM100
├─ Gets: RM5 guaranteed discount
├─ Root earns: RM1 (1%)
└─ Cost to restaurant: 6%

Level 2 (via Level 1) spends RM100
├─ Gets: RM5 guaranteed discount
├─ Level 1 earns: RM1 (1%)
├─ Root earns: RM1 (1%)
└─ Cost to restaurant: 7%

Level 3 (via Level 2) spends RM100
├─ Gets: RM5 guaranteed discount
├─ Level 2 earns: RM1 (1%)
├─ Level 1 earns: RM1 (1%)
├─ Root earns: RM1 (1%)
└─ Cost to restaurant: 8%

Level 4 (via Level 3) spends RM100
├─ Gets: RM5 guaranteed discount
├─ Level 3 earns: RM1 (1%)
├─ Level 2 earns: RM1 (1%)
├─ Level 1 earns: RM1 (1%)
├─ Root earns: RM0 (beyond 3 levels)
└─ Cost to restaurant: 8% (capped)
```

**Key Insight:** Cost per transaction caps at 8% (5% guaranteed + 3% upline rewards), making it predictable and affordable.

## 5. Market Opportunity

### Target Market

**Primary:** Malaysian local restaurants (kopitiam, mamak, cafes, family restaurants)
- **Market Size:** ~50,000 restaurants in Malaysia
- **Target Segment:** 5,000 restaurants in Klang Valley (initial focus)
- **Addressable Market:** Restaurants with RM50,000-500,000 monthly revenue

**Secondary:** Regional chains (2-10 outlets)
- Cross-restaurant functionality (Phase 2) attractive to chains

### Competitive Landscape

| Solution | Pros | Cons | ShareSaji Advantage |
|----------|------|------|---------------------|
| **Traditional Ads** (Facebook, Google) | Wide reach | High cost, low conversion, no loyalty | Pay-for-performance, built-in loyalty |
| **Loyalty Cards** (Stamp cards) | Simple | No virality, manual tracking | Automated, viral, multi-level rewards |
| **Referral Programs** (e.g., GrabFood) | Some virality | Single-level only, high fees (30%) | Multi-level, low cost (8% max) |
| **Discount Apps** (e.g., Fave, eatigo) | Large user base | High commission (10-30%), no ownership | Restaurant owns customer data, lower cost |

**ShareSaji's Differentiation:**
1. **Multi-level rewards** (unique in Malaysia)
2. **No upfront cost** (pay only on sales)
3. **Restaurant owns customer data** (not platform-dependent)
4. **Configurable parameters** (optimize for each restaurant)

## 6. Business Model

### Revenue Streams (Post-MVP)

1. **Freemium Model** (Phase 1-2)
   - Free for single restaurant (MVP pilot)
   - Validate product-market fit

2. **Premium Features** (Phase 3)
   - Advanced analytics (RM99/month)
   - Custom branding (RM49/month)
   - Multi-branch management (RM149/month)

3. **Transaction Fees** (Phase 4)
   - 0.5% fee per transaction (optional, for cross-restaurant network)
   - Example: RM100 transaction → RM0.50 fee

4. **Cross-Restaurant Network** (Phase 4)
   - Restaurants pay RM299/month to join network
   - Benefit: Access to shared customer base (10,000+ users)

### Unit Economics (Per Restaurant)

**Assumptions:**
- Average bill: RM50
- Transactions per month: 500
- Discount rate: 8% average (5% guaranteed + 3% upline)
- Revenue increase: 30% (from new/repeat customers)

**Calculation:**
- **Baseline revenue:** RM25,000/month (500 × RM50)
- **With ShareSaji:** RM32,500/month (+30%)
- **Discount cost:** RM2,600 (RM32,500 × 8%)
- **Net revenue increase:** RM5,000 (RM32,500 - RM25,000 - RM2,600)
- **ROI:** 192% (RM5,000 / RM2,600)

**Conclusion:** Restaurant earns RM5,000 extra per month, making ShareSaji highly attractive.

## 7. Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Single Restaurant Pilot (Weeks 1-4)

**Objective:** Validate product-market fit with one restaurant

**Activities:**
1. Partner with pilot restaurant (ideally 100+ daily customers)
2. Train staff on promotion and checkout flow
3. Place QR codes on tables, posters in restaurant
4. Monitor metrics daily (registrations, transactions, ROI)
5. Collect feedback from customers, staff, owner

**Success Criteria:**
- 100+ registrations in 4 weeks
- 50%+ of customers share their code
- Average 2+ downlines per customer
- Positive ROI for restaurant (20%+ revenue increase)

### Phase 2: Local Expansion (Months 2-3)

**Objective:** Onboard 5-10 restaurants in same area

**Activities:**
1. Leverage pilot restaurant as case study
2. Approach nearby restaurants with ROI data
3. Offer free setup and training
4. Enable cross-restaurant functionality (codes work everywhere)
5. Create local restaurant network

**Success Criteria:**
- 10 restaurants onboarded
- 1,000+ registered customers
- Virality coefficient >1.5 (self-sustaining growth)

### Phase 3: Regional Scale (Months 4-6)

**Objective:** Expand to Klang Valley (50+ restaurants)

**Activities:**
1. Hire 2 sales reps (commission-based)
2. Attend restaurant expos and events
3. Run targeted Facebook ads to restaurants
4. Offer premium features (advanced analytics)
5. Build restaurant community (WhatsApp group, monthly meetups)

**Success Criteria:**
- 50 restaurants onboarded
- 10,000+ registered customers
- RM50,000 monthly recurring revenue (MRR)

## 8. Technology Overview

### Architecture

**Frontend:** React (responsive web app, works on all devices)  
**Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL, auth, storage, Edge Functions)  
**Hosting:** Vercel (frontend), Supabase (backend)  
**Email:** SendGrid (transactional emails)

### Key Technical Features

1. **Multi-Level Referral Engine**
   - Automatic upline chain creation (up to 3 levels)
   - Real-time reward distribution (1% per level)
   - Efficient database queries (indexed referral tree)

2. **Virtual Currency System**
   - Earnings, redemptions, expiry tracking
   - 30-day expiry (configurable)
   - 20% redemption cap per transaction

3. **QR Code System**
   - Generation (react-qr-code)
   - Scanning (html5-qrcode)
   - Fallback to manual entry

4. **Analytics Dashboard**
   - Real-time metrics (transactions, revenue, ROI)
   - Referral tree visualization
   - Top referrers leaderboard

### Scalability

**Current Capacity (Free Tier):**
- 500 users, 1,000 transactions/month
- Sufficient for MVP pilot

**Phase 2 Capacity (Pro Tier - RM125/month):**
- 10,000 users, 50,000 transactions/month
- Sufficient for 10-20 restaurants

**Phase 3 Capacity (Enterprise):**
- 100,000+ users, 500,000+ transactions/month
- Read replicas, CDN, load balancing

## 10. Risk Analysis

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|------------|
| Supabase downtime | High | Low | Monitor uptime, have rollback plan |
| QR scanning issues on iOS | Medium | Medium | Test early, manual entry fallback |
| Referral chain bugs | High | Medium | Unit tests, seed data testing |
| Email deliverability | Medium | Low | Use SendGrid, setup SPF/DKIM |

### Business Risks

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|------------|
| Low restaurant adoption | High | Medium | Strong pilot case study, free trial |
| Poor customer virality | High | Medium | Gamification, social proof, incentives |
| Negative ROI for restaurants | High | Low | Conservative default parameters, monitoring |
| Competitor copycat | Medium | High | First-mover advantage, network effects |

### Operational Risks

| Risk | Impact | Likelihood | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|------------|
| Staff resistance | Medium | Medium | Training, incentives, simple workflow |
| Customer confusion | Medium | Medium | Simple UI, FAQ, staff training |
| Support overload | Low | Low | Comprehensive FAQ, email support |
